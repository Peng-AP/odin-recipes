Scrambled Eggs

Home

Description

A quick, protein-packed breakfast staple made with just eggs, butter, and a pinch of salt.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat with a fork until well mixed.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and add the butter.
  3. Once melted, pour in the eggs.
  4. Stir gently with a spatula, pushing eggs from the edge to the center.
  5. Cook until eggs are just set but still moist.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, then serve.
