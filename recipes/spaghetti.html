Spaghetti

Aglio e Olio

Home

Description

A classic Roman dish that uses just a few pantry staples: spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. It’s quick, cheap, and incredibly flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) spaghetti
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional), chopped
  • Grated Parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté until garlic is golden, not burned.
  4. Add the drained spaghetti to the pan, along with a bit of the reserved pasta water. Toss well to coat.
  5. Cook for another minute, allowing the starch and oil to emulsify.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and Parmesan (if using). Serve immediately.
