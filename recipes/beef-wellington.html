Beef Wellington

Home

Description

A luxurious, centerpiece dish featuring tender beef fillet wrapped in mushroom duxelles, prosciutto, and flaky puff pastry. It requires careful preparation and timing — perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

For the beef:

  • 1 center-cut beef tenderloin (about 2 lb / 900g)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp English mustard (or Dijon)

For the mushroom duxelles:

  • 1 lb (450g) mushrooms (cremini or mixed), finely chopped
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp thyme leaves

For wrapping:

  • 8 slices prosciutto
  • 1 sheet puff pastry (thawed)
  • 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • Flour (for dusting)

Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot skillet with oil until browned on all sides (2–3 min per side).
  3. Remove, let cool slightly, then brush with mustard. Chill in fridge.
  4. In the same pan, melt butter. Add mushrooms, shallots, garlic, and thyme.
  5. Cook until moisture is evaporated and mixture becomes paste-like. Season to taste. Cool completely.
  6. Lay out plastic wrap. Place prosciutto slices overlapping slightly in a rectangle.
  7. Spread mushroom duxelles over prosciutto.
  8. Place beef on top, then tightly roll everything using the plastic wrap. Chill again for 15–30 minutes.
  9. Roll out puff pastry. Remove beef from wrap, place on pastry, and seal tightly.
  10. Brush with egg wash. Optionally, score the top or decorate with pastry cutouts.
  11. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  12. Bake for 35–45 minutes, or until pastry is golden brown and beef is ~130°F (54°C) for medium rare.
  13. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
